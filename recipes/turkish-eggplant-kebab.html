Turkish eggplant kebab

Description

Meatballs and eggplants enriched by garlic and spices come together for a delicious dinner. What’s a better meal? Make this tasty, healthy and filling Eggplant Kebab (Patlican Kebab) for your next dinner. It’ll be a big hit for everyone.

Ingredients

For meatballs

  • 1 pound/450g ground beef, 80% lean
  • 1 onion, finely diced or pureed
  • 2 cloves garlic, mashed
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 and ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

For Eggplant Kebab:

  • 2 eggplants, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 green pepper, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • Parsley for garnish
